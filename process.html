How Our Online Process Works

Tantra Kuti follows a transparent and disciplined online process to ensure clarity, confidentiality, and proper spiritual guidance for seekers across India and internationally.

Step 1: Initial Discussion

The process begins with understanding your concern through WhatsApp, call, or email. This helps in identifying the nature and depth of the issue.

Step 2: Spiritual Assessment

Based on your concern, Kundli analysis or case-based spiritual evaluation is performed to identify the root cause.

Step 3: Sankalp Procedure

A proper sankalp (spiritual intention) is taken in the devotee’s name before beginning any ritual or guidance.

Step 4: Ritual / Guidance Execution

The havan, anushthan, or spiritual process is performed with full discipline, mantra purity, and adherence to traditional विधि-विधान.

Step 5: Completion & Follow-up

After completion, guidance and necessary follow-up instructions are shared to maintain spiritual balance and clarity.
